### Stage 3: Adoption (Days [Y]–Day 90)

*Deepen product usage beyond first value. Goal: customer's whole team is using the product, not just the admin.*

**Exit criterion:** [Describe what "fully adopted" looks like — e.g., ≥ 60% of licensed seats active weekly; all core features configured]

| Timing | Activity | Owner | Type | Goal |
|--------|----------|-------|------|------|
| Day 30 | 30-day business review | CS | Call (60 min) | Review first-value outcomes; expand to team use case; surface any friction |
| Week 5–6 | Feature adoption workshop | CS | Call / Webinar | Train team users (not just admin) on [2–3 core features] |
| Day 60 | Adoption check | CS | Email / Metrics | Review seat activation rate; address non-users |
| Day 90 | 90-day QBR | CS + Customer exec | Call (60 min) | Value delivered review; expansion readiness assessment |

**90-day QBR agenda:**
1. Value delivered (5 min): what outcomes did the customer achieve in 90 days?
2. Usage data review (10 min): adoption metrics, which features are used / not used
3. Goals for next quarter (15 min): what do they want to achieve in Q2?
4. Product roadmap update (10 min): what's coming that's relevant to them?
5. Feedback for us (10 min): what's one thing that would make us more valuable?
6. Next steps (5 min): specific commitments on both sides
