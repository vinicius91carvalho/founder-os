# Job Description Template

> **Purpose:** Write outcome-oriented job descriptions that attract candidates who want to own results — not just complete tasks. Outcome JDs describe what someone will achieve, not what they will do.

---

> **Disclaimer:** This template was generated by an AI system. Validate with your legal counsel that your job descriptions comply with employment law in your jurisdiction before posting. Job descriptions may have legal implications for classification, compensation, and ADA compliance.

---

## Instructions for Using This Template

Before filling this template, answer these pre-work questions:

1. **Why does this role exist?** What problem is so important that you're willing to pay someone $X/year to own it?
2. **What will this person have produced at 90 days?** Be specific. "Set up the sales process" is not an answer. "Closed 10 deals generating $50k ARR and built a repeatable outreach sequence" is.
3. **What would make this person a failure at 6 months?** Write the failure mode. It will clarify what "good" actually means.
4. **What would disqualify a candidate?** (Hard constraints — not preferences.)
