## Part 6: Tone Calibration by Context

> Voice is consistent. Tone adjusts to context. Your voice attributes stay the same; how you apply them shifts.

| Context | Tone adjustment | Example difference |
|---------|----------------|-------------------|
| Error messages | Empathetic + actionable | "Something went wrong. Try again, or contact us at [email]." — not "Error 500: Internal server error." |
| Marketing copy | Confident + direct | Lead with the outcome, not the feature |
| Customer success / support | Warm + solution-oriented | Validate first, then solve |
| Social media | Conversational + opinionated | Shorter sentences; stronger point of view; personal opinions welcome |
| Press releases | Formal (strategic exception) | Standard PR format; our voice applies to quotes only |
| Investor communications | Precise + data-forward | Numbers first; plain language explanations |
