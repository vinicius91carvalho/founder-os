**SEO strategy for early-stage startups:**
- Target keywords with difficulty < 40 first (you will not outrank established sites on high-difficulty keywords)
- Long-tail keywords (4+ words) have lower volume but higher intent and easier to rank for
- One definitive piece per keyword — don't split focus
- Update and republish pieces every 6–12 months; Google rewards freshness
