## Usage Guidelines

1. **Minimum requirement:** Every skill output and artifact MUST include at least the General Disclaimer.
2. **Domain-specific:** Add the relevant domain disclaimer when content touches financial, legal, or tax topics.
3. **Placement:** Disclaimers appear at the end of the artifact, before "Next Steps" and "Related Artifacts."
4. **Prominence:** In high-risk domains (legal, financial, tax), include a brief inline warning at the top of the relevant section: *"Note: This section is educational. Consult a qualified [lawyer/accountant/tax professional] for advice specific to your situation."*
5. **Combination:** When multiple disclaimers apply, include all relevant ones. The General Disclaimer always comes first.
