## Launch Failure Recovery Plan

If launch produces < 20% of signups target after 72 hours:

**Step 1 — Diagnose**
- Check: Did the right audience see it? (Check referral sources)
- Check: Did they understand the product? (Test the headline with 5 strangers)
- Check: Was there a technical issue that blocked signups? (Review drop-off analytics)

**Step 2 — Identify the breakpoint**
- If visits are low (< 100): distribution problem — not enough people saw it
- If visits are high, signups are low: messaging problem — people saw it but didn't understand or care
- If signups are high, activations are low: onboarding problem — people wanted it but couldn't get value

**Step 3 — Run a second launch within 30 days**
- One bad launch day is not a failed company
- Fix the identified breakpoint
- Launch again in a different community or at a different time
- Use the first launch as the story: "Since we launched [X] days ago, here's what we've learned…" — iteration makes the second launch more credible, not less
