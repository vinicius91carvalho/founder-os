# Burn Rate Calculator

> **Purpose:** Calculate your gross and net burn rate with line-item precision. Know exactly where your money is going so you can manage it deliberately.

---

> **Financial Disclaimer:** The calculations in this template are based on inputs you provide. They do not constitute financial advice. Actual results will vary. Consult a qualified accountant before making significant financial decisions.

*Note: This template is educational. Consult a qualified accountant for advice specific to your situation.*

---

## Instructions

1. Fill in your company name, period, and opening/closing cash balances from your bank statement (not your accounting software — bank statements are ground truth)
2. List every cash expense by category with the actual monthly amount
3. List every cash receipt by source
4. The template will calculate your gross burn, net burn, and burn trend

**Use cash basis, not accrual:** This calculator tracks when cash actually moves, not when revenue is recognized or expenses are incurred. A $120k annual software contract paid upfront counts in the month it clears your bank.
