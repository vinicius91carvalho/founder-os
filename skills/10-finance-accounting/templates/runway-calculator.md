# Runway Calculator — Scenario Analysis

> **Purpose:** Calculate your runway under multiple scenarios so you can make decisions based on realistic projections, not just current state. The goal is to always know your worst-case runway and act accordingly.

---

> **Financial Disclaimer:** Runway projections are estimates based on stated assumptions. Actual results will vary materially based on business performance, market conditions, and execution. This does not constitute financial advice. Consult a qualified accountant or financial advisor before making decisions based on these projections.

*Note: This template is educational. Consult a qualified accountant for advice specific to your situation.*

---

## Instructions

1. Fill in Section 1 (current state) from your most recent bank reconciliation and P&L
2. Fill in Section 2 (assumptions) for each scenario — be honest about what you're actually planning
3. The runway calculations in Section 3 will show how long your money lasts under each scenario
4. Use Section 4 to identify the "fundraise trigger" — when you need to start a raise to avoid a cash crisis

**Key rule:** When making significant decisions (hiring, signing office lease, starting a raise), use the Conservative scenario, not the Base case.
