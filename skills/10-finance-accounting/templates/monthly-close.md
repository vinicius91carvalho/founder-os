# Monthly Close Checklist

> **Purpose:** Complete the books within 5 business days of each month end. A disciplined monthly close ensures you always know your financial position and prevents the compounding errors that make quarterly closes a nightmare.

---

> **Financial Disclaimer:** This checklist is a guide. The specific steps required for your close will vary based on your accounting method, entity type, and business model. Consult a qualified accountant before implementing your close process.

*Note: This checklist is educational. Consult a qualified accountant for advice specific to your situation.*

## Step 1: Bank Reconciliation

*Confirm every transaction in your accounting software matches your bank statement. The bank statement is ground truth — any discrepancy means your books are wrong.*

- [ ] Download bank statement for [Month YYYY] (all accounts)
- [ ] Reconcile checking account: all transactions matched, statement balance = book balance

- [ ] Reconcile payroll account (if separate)
- [ ] **Outcome:** Ending book balance matches ending bank balance for each account
